About Us

Well Established music school that specialises in singing and various musical instruments. Competitive prices and flexible working hours to suit all types of client. Classes for beginners and advanced. All ages and abilities welcome.

Our Team

Caitlin McGillivray Singing/Piano

Caitlin studied singing from the age of ten with Fiona Mitchell and continued with her throughout high school. She then studied with Joan Busby during her time at Edinburgh Napier University, where she graduated in 2016. Caitlin has worked at Mansfield School of Music in Musselburgh for the last 8 years, teaching piano and singing and has previously served as Musical Director for three shows with amateur musical theatre group Our Lady of Loretto Youth Players.
